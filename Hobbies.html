My Hobbies

My favourite hobby is cross stitching which I have been doing since I was a young person. At the moment, I am doing houses with snow on top of it which is very enjoyable. I like reading books like Tess Gerritsen and J.K Rowling and Rebecca Yarros as well as autobiographies as I like to learn about people from the person themselves. I like to watch TV shows, for example, sci-fi and fantasy as well as crime shows. Furthermore, I like to write short stories but at the moment I am writing about myself about things that have happened to me. Finally, I like watching sports like football, tennis and darts.
